I am a designer, educator, and researcher based in Bristol, UK. Through my pracice I explore material relations with digital technology. I explore this through a number of of process including creative workshops, developing curriulum, game making, and desgining DIY sensors.

In the past few years I have been resident in a number of schools, museums, and arts spaces. I worked on the Brisol Approach with Knowle West Media Centre who I continue to collaborate with.Exploratorium in San Francisco. I was awared an Art+Technology grant by the Arts Countil England to work at Mackervsitry Somerset House in London to develop tactile sensing. I traveled to Wired Lab in Tokyo to work on a Playable Cities collaboration. Currently, I am a resident artist at the Pervasive Media Studio and I am co-curating art and technology festival Control Shift.

In 2018 I recived an AHRC studendship for PhD reseach at Goldsmiths, where I am exploring learning and technology. My focus is on developing critical technology education through DIY sensing practices.
