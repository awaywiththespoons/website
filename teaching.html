2020 ~ Assosiate Lecturer on MA Design
Goldsmiths, University of London (UK)

I am currently a graduate-tutor at Goldsmiths. I teach on the "Transfocality" module, which is part of the MA Design degree that shifts the focus of design accross disciplines and contexts. I work with Jennifer Bain and Danialla Z on the trans-focal area of designing and learning, and worked with them to design the module "Narrow Corridors". Drawing from criical pedaggogies (hooks 1994, Freire 1970) students developed a radical response to an issue. Find out more.
