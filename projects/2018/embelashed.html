Embelashed

The Pocket Connectors are how paper circuits can easily connect to PCB circuitry and the Bela Mini. A different connector is used for different types of circuits/p>

The Paper Capelet is a circuit board that attaches on top of the Bela Mini. It connects analog and digital pins on the Bela Mini to white Grove connectors. Grove connector cables can then be used to connect to sensors using Pocket Connectors.

Bela is a platform for creating responsive interactive musical instruments. I worked with a team at Queen Mary, University of London, to develop “Embelashed” – a toolkit for protopying interactive wearables. In the project my role was to design paper circuitry systems that can be used by people wanting to make quick and simple sensors with the Bela Mini (lead by Becky Stewart). I also worked on the designs for the “paper caplet” which is a circuit board that attaches to the Bela Mini to provides electrical connections with paper sensors.
